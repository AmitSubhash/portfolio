Projects

Diffuse Optical Tomography

  • piglet-realtime — Real-time multi-wavelength HbO/HbR DOT reconstruction for neonatal brain imaging.
  • diff-photon-transport — Differentiable Monte Carlo photon transport in JAX/PyTorch.
  • diffusion-priors-dot — Diffusion posterior sampling priors for DOT inverse reconstruction.
  • dot-test-time-compute — o1-style test-time compute scaling for DOT reconstruction.
  • dot-eval-harness — Versioned evaluation suite for DOT reconstruction pipelines.
  • dspy-dot-rag — DSPy + ColBERT RAG over the DOT and fNIRS literature.

Preclinical neuroimaging (piglet)

  • synpig — Autonomous piglet brain extraction with SynthSeg-style domain randomization (Dice 0.9622 on 4-week piglet skull stripping).
  • sam-synthseg-piglet — SAM2 + SynthSeg-style domain randomization for contrast-agnostic piglet brain segmentation.
  • PigSeg — Piglet brain segmentation pipeline.
  • PUMBA-Piglet — Piglet brain analysis pipeline.
  • DL-PigletBrainExtraction — 2.5D deep learning skull-stripping pipeline (PigBET).

Interpretability and ML systems

  • biosignal-interp — Sparse-autoencoder mechanistic interpretability on a tiny fNIRS transformer.
  • MathforNeuro — Survey of mathematical frameworks in neuroimaging.
